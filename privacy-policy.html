Privacy Policy – KisanKheti Platform

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

KisanKheti respects the privacy of its users and sellers and is committed to protecting the personal information shared on the platform. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and shared on the KisanKheti application and website.

2. Information We Collect

At present, listings and advertisements on KisanKheti are uploaded by the KisanKheti team on behalf of users/sellers. Information may be collected through phone calls, WhatsApp, or direct communication.

  • Seller name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Location (village, taluka, district, state)
  • Machine or product details
  • Photos of machines or products

3. Consent from Sellers

By sharing information with KisanKheti, sellers provide explicit consent to publish their name, contact number, location, price, and photos, and to promote listings on KisanKheti and its marketing channels.

4. Use of Information

  • Publish listings and advertisements
  • Connect buyers and sellers
  • Promote listings on social media
  • Improve platform services
  • Provide customer support

5. Data Protection

KisanKheti does not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.

6. Buyer Responsibility

Buyers must independently verify seller identity, machine condition, ownership, documents, and price before making any transaction.

7. Policy Changes

KisanKheti reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.
