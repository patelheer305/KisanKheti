Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

KisanKheti respects the privacy of its users and sellers and is committed to protecting personal information shared with us.

Information We Collect

KisanKheti collects information directly from sellers through phone calls, WhatsApp, or other communication methods. This includes name, mobile number, location, price, machine details, and photos.

How Information Is Used

  • To publish machinery listings on the platform
  • To connect buyers and sellers
  • To promote listings on KisanKheti and social media
  • To provide customer support

Consent

By sharing information with KisanKheti, sellers give explicit consent to publish their details and photos on the platform.

Data Protection

KisanKheti does not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.

Buyer Responsibility

Buyers must independently verify seller details, machine condition, and documents before making any transaction.

Policy Changes

KisanKheti may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the platform means acceptance of the updated policy.
